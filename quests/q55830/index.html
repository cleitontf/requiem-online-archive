Description
This airship was used as a Cruise Ship for noble people during Zudekha age. When Thanatos went berserk, it crashed here. Ioxenic came here to explore, but the upper part is occupied by sea monsters, so it's impossible to get in. After taking out all the water, just the lower part became accessible. However, a lot of ghosts and spectors suddenly appeared making the exploration difficult. I think they are ghosts of sacrificed passengers. Requiem ritual performed by the Xenon Order can clear out these ghosts. But, in order to do this ritual, we need to get the belongings of these ghosts. Can you help us get those?

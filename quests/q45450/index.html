A youngster like you probably doesn't know that all this land was once ruled by the Xenon Empire. Not the one we have today, mind you, but a much worse one. Everyone was robbed of their natural rights to freedom, and we were all put to hard, grueling work for the glory of the Empire. Yes, they forced people to mine the Stone Pit, telling us that it was to obtain Aniyass, but they actually wanted to excavate the Ancient Relics there. Those ancient artifacts would bring the Empire even more power. Then, they discovered one particular relic that was... It was both terrible and wonderful.

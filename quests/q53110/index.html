Description
The Keepsakes of the Kun Soul Siphoner are sending out unknown energy. Several things have then come to mind; things to be done about it, shown in my head quite clearly. Please defeat Qualin and bring the keepsakes of Soul Siphoner to the Totem of Control to purify it.

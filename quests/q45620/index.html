Description
There are different kinds of Blacksmiths that provide you with a variety of services, as you can see. Reduction Blacksmiths like me can reduce items that you've obtained from hunting monsters into Xeons. Compound Blacksmiths can compound items for you, and then there are the Blacksmiths that specialize in repairing the duration of your items. If you want to bestow attributes for your items, you shoud speak to an Enchant Blacksmith. Make sure that you visit the kind of blacksmith that provides the specific service you need. Oh, and feel free to come and talk to me whenever you want me to destroy and reduce your weapons. Hahaha! Here we are! I've stamped your paper. Now, please visit Merice Bank to talk with Clerk Tanasa. Don't worry, you're almost finished with this little errand the mayor gave you.

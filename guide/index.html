Game Guide

The World of Ethergia

Ethergia is a world ravaged by the abuse of science and dark magic. Millennia of war between ancient races left the land corrupted, its people scarred, and its history written in blood. Four races survived the collapse of the Holy Xenon Empire — each carrying old wounds, old grudges, and their own path forward.

This guide covers the four playable races, their base classes, and the sixteen subclasses available in Requiem Online. Each subclass links directly to its skill calculator.

Races

Turan
Characteristics Turans are the representative race of Ethergia. Although they have no distinctively outstanding strengths, they are renowned for their tenacity and solidarity. A rational race that upholds justice, they prefer balanced and harmonized combat over brute force, excelling in agility and adaptability. They adopted divine magic from the Xenoa during the era of the Xenon Empire.
Strength / Weakness Balanced strength, agility, and intelligence / Low resistance against magic
Relationship with Other Races Allied historically with Bartuk. Naturally opposed to Kruxena and weak to dark magic attacks. During the Xenon Empire era, Turans developed divine magic as a counterbalance to their physical limitations.
Base classes Defender · Templar
Bartuk
Characteristics Bartuks are gifted with enormous physical power, but tend to be spiritually weak. They are easily enraged against their enemies — a weakness sometimes exploited to make them lose control. Generous to their allies and merciless to their enemies, their decision-making is simple: protect the friend, destroy the enemy. Their magic, limited to black arts and fire, is unfamiliar to other races and difficult to counter.
Strength / Weakness High strength and solidarity / Low spirit, susceptible minds
Relationship with Other Races Deep-seated hatred toward Xenoa due to historical domination, but have learned to exploit Xenoa weaknesses. Can cooperate with Kruxena. Loathe Turans.
Base classes Warrior · Shaman
Kruxena
Characteristics Created by the ancient Natsu race as living weapons, Kruxena carry that origin in their nature. Endowed with both physical and spiritual power, they can use short and long range weapons and are skillful in magic. Brutal, cold-hearted, and antisocial, they will sacrifice companions to accomplish their ends and have no qualms about mutilating enemies.
Strength / Weakness Physical and spiritual superiority / Won't cooperate with other races; lack compassion and tenacity; inhumane
Relationship with Other Races Unfriendly to all races except Bartuk, with whom they share genetic roots from the Natsu era. Their contempt starkly contrasts with the solidarity of the Turans.
Base classes Rogue · Soul Hunter
Xenoa
Characteristics A minority race of unmatched intelligence. Xenoa compensate for their small, physically weak bodies with advanced long-range attacks, support magic, healing, and detection abilities. Their culture is ordered by religion, faith, and authority. They believe themselves superior to other races — and have the wisdom to hide it. A Xenoa would sacrifice itself to cast a single devastating spell.
Strength / Weakness High intelligence, swiftness, and visual precision / Small body, very low physical strength
Relationship with Other Races Tend to disregard other races but are skilled at concealing this. Expert manipulators with unmatched magical ability. Bartuk harbor deep historical resentment toward them.
Base classes Hunter · Battle Magician

Classes & Subclasses

Turan
Defender → Commander / Protector
Commander
Battlefield leader. Buffs allies, demoralizes enemies, strikes multiple targets. Can teleport across the battlefield.
Protector
Support tank. Fewer raw stats than Commander, but greater party protection through buffs and heals.
Templar → Tempest / Radiant
Tempest
Electrical mage with self-healing. Hits multiple targets, slows enemies with lightning.
Radiant
Ultimate healer. Restores HP and MP; can silence enemies; has close-range melee spells.
Bartuk
Warrior → Warlord / Berserker
Warlord
Two-handed weapon specialist focused on buffs and debuffs. Great group utility over raw damage.
Berserker
Two-handed damage dealer with a rage mechanic. Massive critical hits; dashes at enemies in a burst of fire.
Shaman → Forsaker / Mystic
Forsaker
Melee-caster hybrid. Fire magic, healing, and defensive boons; summons healing spirits for the party.
Mystic
Offensive spellcaster. Devastating fire magic with powerful AoE spells.
Kruxena
Rogue → Assassin / Shadow Runner
Assassin
Exploits enemy weaknesses with poisons and positional attacks. Can deal AoE damage.
Shadow Runner
Ranged rogue. Poisons, interrupts spellcasting, and delivers explosive damage from a distance.
Soul Hunter → Defiler / Dominator
Defiler
Ranged curser. Masters DoTs, long-distance AoEs, and debuffs to support the party.
Dominator
Necromancer. Summons and strengthens undead minions that deal magic damage and heal their master.
Xenoa
Hunter → Ranger / Avenger
Ranger
Pure ranged attacker. Increases ranged damage, causes bleeds and confusion, detects stealthed enemies.
Avenger
Ranged debuffer. Captures monsters as pets; deploys traps against enemies.
Battle Magician → Elementalist / Druid
Elementalist
Highest magical damage in the game. Powerful elemental spells and strong party buffs, especially in PvP.
Druid
Master of transformation. Shifts into Doomguard (tank) or Infernal (melee DPS); must specialize in one form.
